Housing Disrepair Claims: Get the Repairs & Compensation You Deserve

If you are living in a council or housing association property that has fallen into poor condition, Housing Disrepair Support Line is here to help. What Counts as Housing Disrepair?

Housing disrepair covers any serious issue that affects the safety, comfort, or health of your household. Common issues include:

Leaks and Water Damage

Including roof leaks, burst pipes, and faulty guttering

Mould and Damp

Which can cause respiratory problems and long-term damage to your home

Electrical Faults

Unsafe wiring, or heating systems that do not work properly

Structural Problems

Such as cracked walls, unstable floors, or broken windows and doors

Infestations

Including rodents or insects caused by property defects

Heating Issues

Broken boilers, radiators, or heating systems

Severe Water Damage & Mould

Ceiling damage with extensive mould growth and moisture readings showing unsafe conditions
